TELEPRESENCE LIVE LECTURE PORTAL

Africa Centre of Excellence in ICT, OAK PARK

Coordinating Unit — Faculty of Computing Science & Engineering

The Telepresence Live Lecture Portal marks an important milestone in our digital teaching strategy at Obafemi Awolowo University. By bringing high-quality, synchronous lectures into students’ homes and study spaces, we strengthen teaching continuity, broaden access to expert instruction, and develop resilient learning pathways across faculties.

- Prof. G. A. Aderounmu

Centre Leader — Africa Centre of Excellence in ICT, OAK PARK

Our Faculty embraces this Telepresence initiative as a strategic opportunity to enhance learning experiences and academic reach for all faculties. Students across departments and faculties are encouraged to engage actively with live lectures, register their attendance, review archived resources, and make full use of the digital tools provided to support their academic success.

- Prof. E. A. Olajubu

Dean — Faculty of Computing Science & Engineering
