TELEPRESENCE LIVE LECTURE PORTAL

Africa Centre of Excellence

Coordinating Unit: Faculty of Computing Science and Engineering

The Telepresence Live Lecture Portal marks a major transformation in remote learning and academic collaboration. By leveraging modern telepresence and streaming infrastructure, students across faculties can access synchronous lectures, interact with instructors, and retrieve archived materials for review and study.

This platform supports resilient teaching and learning, enabling uninterrupted academic delivery, virtual collaboration, and improved access to subject-matter expertise. Students are encouraged to participate actively and use the portal's resources.
