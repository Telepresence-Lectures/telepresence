TELEPRESENCE LIVE LECTURE PORTAL

Africa Centre of Excellence in ICT, OAK PARK

Coordinating Unit: Faculty of Computing Science and Engineering

The Telepresence Live Lecture Portal marks a major transformation in remote learning and academic collaboration at our Centre. By leveraging high-performance visualization, modern teleconferencing infrastructure, and virtual presence technology, this portal ensures that students across departments and faculties access their lectures in real time, without barriers or delays.

As you explore this platform, we encourage all students to take full advantage of the improved delivery method. The system is designed to support seamless learning, promote engagement, and extend access beyond physical lecture spaces. Commit yourself to using this platform regularly for a better academic experience.
