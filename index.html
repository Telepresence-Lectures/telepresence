The Telepresence Live Lecture Portal marks a significant advancement in remote academic delivery, allowing lectures to be streamed live with high clarity and real-time interaction.

This platform expands the boundaries of digital learning, enabling seamless communication between lecturers and students across distant locations, while preserving classroom engagement quality.

  • Postgraduate Virtual Oral Exams
  • Virtual Symposia, Workshop and Conference Hosting
  • Virtual Research Collaborations
  • Presentation Layer for CPU-Intensive Simulations
  • Data Storage Facilities at Telepresence Data Center
